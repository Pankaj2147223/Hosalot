Hostel Allotment System

Abstract

Hosalot (Hosalot management system) is a tool that makes it easier for the University to implement an automatic system that decides and allots University hostel room. Students who are enrolled in the University can book either Sharing or Private room as per their convenience.

The system

Hosalot (Hosalot management system) tool takes in various factors like the course that the student is enrolled in. It also takes into account the graduation level. The system then allots the room(if available) to the student. After every year the cycle changes and the rooms and students allotted in the upward directions with better facilities.
